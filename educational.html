RECYCLING

RECYCLING

A Process of converting waste materials into reusable msterials to prevent their disposal as conventional waste. Recycling reduces the consumption of new raw materials, energy usage, and greenhouse gas emissions. It promotes sustainability by conserving resources and mitigating environment impacts of waste disposal.

COMPOSTING

Com - posting

Natural decomposition of organic waste, such as food scraps and yard trimminggs, to create nutrient-rich soil conditioner for gardening and landscaping.

BIN ROTATION

BIN ROTATION

A waste collection strategy invloving the rotation of different types of bins or containers(e.g. general waste, recycling, organic waste) for curbside collection on scheduled days, encouraging residents to separate and dispose of waste properly.

GREEN BIN PROGRAM

GREEN BIN PROGRAM

A waste management initiative implemented by municipalities to encourage residents to separate and compost organic waste, such as food scraps and yard trimmings, using designated green bins or containers for collection and composting.

HOUSEHOLD HAZARDOUS WASTE

HOUSEHOLD HAZARDOUS WASTE

Potentially harmful or toxic waste materials commonly found in households, including household cleaners, paints, batteries, and pesticides, which require special handling and disposal to prevent environmental contamination and health risks.

Community Recycling Events

ACommunity Recycling Events

Organized events or initiatives held by municipalities or community organizations to promote recycling and proper waste disposal practices among residents, including electronic waste recycling drives, paper shredding events, and household hazardous waste drop-off days.

PAY-AS-YOU-THROW

PAY-AS-YOU-THROW

A waste management pricing system where households are charged based on the amount of waste they generate or dispose of, incentivizing waste reduction, recycling, and composting while discouraging excessive waste production.

HOUSEHOLD WASTE AUDIT

HOUSEHOLD WASTE AUDIT

A self-assessment or evaluation conducted by households to analyze their waste generation habits, identify areas for improvement, and implement effective waste reduction, recycling, and composting practices tailored to their needs and lifestyle.
